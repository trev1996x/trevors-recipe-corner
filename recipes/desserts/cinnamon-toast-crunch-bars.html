Cinnamon Toast Crunch Bars

Deliciously sweet and crunchy bars made with Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 16 bars

Ingredients & Supplies

  • 4 1/2 cups Cinnamon Toast Crunch
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cups mini marshmallows
  • 11x7 or 9x9 inch baking pan

Instructions

  1. Spray large mixing bowl with cooking spray; add cereal and set aside.
  2. Heat butter and marshmallows in large heavy-bottom saucepan over medium heat until mixture is melted and smooth, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from heat and cool 5 minutes; pour marshmallow mixture over cereal, and mix well to coat.
  4. Press mixture into greased full sheet pan. (Place a piece of parchment paper on top of mixture, and press with a rolling pin to assist.)
  5. Cool at room temperature approx. 1 hour before portioning.
