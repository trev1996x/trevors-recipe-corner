Mashed Potatoes

Creamy and fluffy mashed potatoes made with butter and creamâ€”perfect as a side dish for any meal.

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients & Supplies

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Seasonings: garlic salt, pepper, onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  3. Add butter, milk, and minced garlic to the potatoes.
  4. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher or electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with garlic salt, pepper, and onion powder to taste. Mix well.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

Tips & Notes

Add the milk in slowly. Depending on the size of the potatoes used, you may not need all of the milk. Add additional milk if potatoes are still too thick.
