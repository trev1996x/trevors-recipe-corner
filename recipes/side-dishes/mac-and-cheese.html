Tini's Mac and Cheese

This recipe is so simple and basic, there is NO excuse to buy that nasty store bought canned sauce.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes
Servings: ~12 servings

Ingredients & Supplies

  • 1 package (10-12 oz.) cavatappi pasta
  • 1 block mozarella cheese
  • 1 block colby jack cheese
  • 1 block sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • Seasonings:
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook cavatappi according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Combine all 3 shredded cheeses in a bowl and divide half into another bowl. Set aside
  4. Combine garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Divide in half and set aside.
  5. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Once melted, add half of the season mixture and flour. Stir until the mixture becomes paste-like, about 3-5 minutes.
  6. Add evaporated milk and whisk until thick.
  7. Add heavy cream and the rest of the seasoning mixture and mix until combined.
  8. Slowly add in half the cheese, allowing it to melt before adding more.
  9. Stir in cavatappi until coated with cheese sauce.
  10. Add a layer of cavatappi and then a layer of shredded cheese to prepared baking dish. Repeat layers.
  11. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 25-30 min.
  12. Finish by broiling until top is golden brown, about 2 min.

Tips & Notes

Tini's recipe does not originally call for onion powder, however, I added it to this recipe since I like onion powder.

This receipe also has an optional 1 tablespoon of dijon mustard that may be added after step 7. I do not include this.
