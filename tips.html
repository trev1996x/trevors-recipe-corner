Tips & Glossary

Kitchen Essentials

Must-have tools and equipment for every kitchen:

  • Japanese Santoku knife
  • Kitchenaid mixer
  • Bamboo cutting board
  • Kitchen scale
  • Cast iron skillet
  • Non-stick frying pan (I personally love Gotham Steel)
  • Small portion bowls for mise en place
  • Ninja food processor

Mise en Place

A French culinary phrase which means "everything in its place." It refers to setting out all your ingredients, chopped and measured, before you start cooking. This makes the cooking process smoother and more organized.

Al Dente

Italian for "to the tooth," this term describes pasta that is cooked until it is firm but not hard. It should have a slight bite. Check your pasta a minute or two before the package instructions say it will be done.

EVOO

Extra Virgin Olive Oil
